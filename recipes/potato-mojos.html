Crispy Potato Mojos

Description

Crispy Potato Mojos! Super easy and yummy snack or appetizer for your next get-together. Potatoes dipped in fried chicken mix = AMAZING.

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes, washed
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups fried chicken breading mix, like Shore Lunch or Oven Fry brand
  • Oil for frying
